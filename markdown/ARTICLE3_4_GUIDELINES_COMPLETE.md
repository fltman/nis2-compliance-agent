## Purpose
Provides guidance on:

2. **Registration template** - Standard format for entities to submit required information

## Legal Basis
- **NIS2 Directive (EU) 2022/2555** - Article 3(4)
- Empowers Commission to provide guidelines on establishing entity lists
# Article 3(4) Guidelines - Introduction

## I. Entity List Requirements

### Article 3(3) Obligation
Member States must establish a **list of essential and important entities** as well as entities providing domain name registration services.

### Key Deadline
**By 17 April 2025**, Member States must:
1. Establish the entity list
2. Review and update **regularly** (at least every **two years**)

### Article 27(2) Registration
Entities must submit the following information to competent authorities:
- Name of entity
- Address and contact details
- Sector, subsector, entity type
- List of Member States where services are provided
- IP ranges and relevant contact information

---

## II. Purpose of These Guidelines

### Why Entity Lists Matter
- Enable **effective supervision** of NIS2 compliance
- Allow authorities to identify which entities fall under NIS2 scope
- Support cross-border coordination (know which entities operate in multiple Member States)

### Commission's Role
Article 3(4) empowers the Commission to adopt guidelines providing:
- **Template** for information submission
- Guidance on **scope assessment** by sector/subsector

---

## III. Implementation Approach

### Member State Flexibility
Member States may:
- Use existing registration systems
- Develop new dedicated systems
- Require additional information beyond the template

### Minimum Requirements
All systems must collect at least the information specified in the **Annex template** to ensure cross-border interoperability.

# Article 3(4) Guidelines - Registration Template Fields

## Required Information for Entity Registration

### 1. Entity Identification

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Name** | Legal name of the entity | Yes |
| **Address** | Registered business address | Yes |
| **Registration number** | National business/company registration | Yes |

---

### 2. Contact Information

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Email** | Primary contact email | Yes |
| **Phone** | Primary contact phone | Yes |
| **Contact person** | Name of responsible person | Yes |

---

### 3. Sector Classification

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Sector** | Main sector (from Annex I or II) | Yes |
| **Subsector** | Specific subsector within main sector | Yes |
| **Entity type** | Specific type of entity | Yes |

---

### 4. Cross-Border Information

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Member States** | List of all EU Member States where entity provides services | Yes |
| **Main establishment** | Primary Member State for NIS2 purposes | Yes |

---

### 5. Technical Information

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **IP address ranges** | Public IP ranges used by entity | If applicable |
| **Domain names** | Domain names owned/operated | If applicable |

---

## Classification Reminder

### Essential vs Important Entity
- **Essential**: Size-cap applies (>250 employees OR >€50M turnover AND >€43M balance sheet) OR specific designation
- **Important**: All other in-scope entities not meeting essential criteria

### Size-Cap Exceptions
Certain entity types are **always essential** regardless of size:
- Trust service providers (qualified)
- TLD registries
- DNS service providers
- Public electronic communications providers
- Public administration entities
- Sole providers of critical services in Member State
