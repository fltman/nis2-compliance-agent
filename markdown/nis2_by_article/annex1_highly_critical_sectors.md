# NIS2 Directive - ANNEX I: Sectors of High Criticality

These sectors contain **essential entities** (if large) or **important entities** (if medium).

---

## 6. DRINKING WATER
- Suppliers and distributors of water for human consumption

**Exclusion**: Distributors where water distribution is non-essential part of other activity.

---

## 7. WASTE WATER
- Undertakings collecting, disposing of, or treating urban/domestic/industrial waste water

**Exclusion**: Where waste water treatment is non-essential part of general activity.

## 10. PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION
- Central government entities (as defined by Member State)
- Regional level entities (as defined by Member State)

**Note**: Excludes judiciary, parliaments, central banks, national security/defence entities.

---

## 11. SPACE
- Operators of ground-based infrastructure supporting space-based services

**Exclusion**: Providers of public electronic communications networks.
