# NIS2 Directive - Articles 40-46: Final Provisions

## Article 40: Review

### First Review
**17 October 2027**

### Subsequent Reviews
Every **36 months** thereafter

### Review Scope
Commission shall assess:
- Relevance of **entity size** thresholds
- **Sectors, subsectors, and entity types** in Annexes I and II
- Functioning of economy and society in relation to cybersecurity

May be accompanied by legislative proposal if necessary.

---

## Article 41: Transposition

| Deadline | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **17 October 2024** | Member States adopt and publish national measures |
| **18 October 2024** | Member States **apply** those measures |

---

## Article 42: Amendment of eIDAS Regulation
**Regulation (EU) No 910/2014** (eIDAS): Article 19 **deleted** from 18 October 2024.

## Article 43: Amendment of EECC Directive
**Directive (EU) 2018/1972** (European Electronic Communications Code): Articles 40 and 41 **deleted** from 18 October 2024.

---

## Article 44: Repeal

**NIS1 Directive** (EU) 2016/1148 is **repealed** from **18 October 2024**.

References to NIS1 shall be construed as references to NIS2 per **Annex III** correlation table.

---

## Article 45: Entry into Force
NIS2 enters into force on the **twentieth day** following Official Journal publication.

## Article 46: Addressees
This Directive is addressed to the **Member States**.
