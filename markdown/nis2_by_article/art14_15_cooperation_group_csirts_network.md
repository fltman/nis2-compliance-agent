# NIS2 Directive - Articles 14 & 15: Cooperation Group & CSIRTs Network

## Article 14: Cooperation Group

### Article 14(1): Purpose
Facilitate strategic cooperation and information exchange among Member States.

### Article 14(2): Meetings
At least once per year with **European Parliament** and relevant stakeholders.

### Article 14(3): Composition
| Role | Participants |
|------|--------------|
| **Members** | Member State representatives, Commission, ENISA |
| **Observers** | European External Action Service, ESAs/DORA authorities |
| **Invitees** | European Parliament, relevant stakeholders |
| **Secretariat** | Commission |

### Article 14(4): Main Tasks

| Task | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **(a)** | Guide competent authorities on NIS2 implementation |
| **(b)** | Guide on coordinated vulnerability disclosure policies |
| **(c)** | Exchange best practices (threats, incidents, training, skills) |
| **(d)** | Advise Commission on emerging cybersecurity policy |
| **(e)** | Input on draft delegated/implementing acts |
| **(f)** | Information exchange with EU institutions |
| **(g)** | Review sector-specific cybersecurity law implementation |
| **(h)** | Discuss peer review reports, draw conclusions |
| **(i)** | Conduct **coordinated supply chain risk assessments** (Article 22) |
| **(j)** | Non-binding guidance on entity identification criteria |
| **(k)** | Exchange on capacity building and cybersecurity exercises |
| **(l)** | Coordinate on mutual assistance |
| **(m)** | Exchange on national/EU research and innovation |
| **(n)** | Contribute to cooperation with third countries and international bodies |

## Article 15: CSIRTs Network

### Article 15(1): Purpose
Contribute to development of confidence and trust and promote swift and effective operational cooperation.

### Article 15(2): Composition
| Role | Participants |
|------|--------------|
| **Members** | National CSIRTs, CERT-EU |
| **Observers** | ENISA, Commission |
| **Secretariat** | ENISA |
| **Chair** | Member State holding Council Presidency |

### Article 15(3): Tasks

| Task | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **(a)** | Exchange on CSIRT services, operations, cooperation |
| **(b)** | Exchange on incident-related information |
| **(c)** | Support **voluntary mutual assistance** |
| **(d)** | Analyse incident reports and early warnings |
| **(e)** | **Coordinated response** to cross-border incidents |
| **(f)** | Discuss common approach on incident types |
| **(g)** | Assist Member States on cross-border incidents |
| **(h)** | Identify coordinated response needs |
| **(i)** | Contribute to deployment of secure information-sharing tools |
| **(j)** | Participate in joint exercises and training |
| **(k)** | Exchange on operational cooperation with third parties |
| **(l)** | Develop contribution for Commission reports |

### Article 15(4): Reports
Every **two years**: Report to Cooperation Group on operational cooperation progress, conclusions, recommendations.
