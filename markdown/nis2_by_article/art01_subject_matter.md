# NIS2 Directive - Article 1: Subject Matter

## Main Objective
Achieve a **high common level of cybersecurity across the EU** to improve the functioning of the internal market.

## Four Key Pillars

### 1. National Framework Obligations
Member States must adopt national cybersecurity strategies and designate/establish:
- Competent authorities
- Cyber crisis management authorities
- Single points of contact on cybersecurity
- CSIRTs (Computer Security Incident Response Teams)

### 2. Risk Management & Reporting
- Cybersecurity risk-management measures for entities in Annex I or II
- Reporting obligations for these entities
- Also applies to critical entities under Directive (EU) 2022/2557

### 3. Information Sharing
Rules for cybersecurity information sharing between entities

### 4. Supervision & Enforcement
Member States must supervise and enforce compliance
