# NIS2 Directive - Article 6: Definitions

All 41 definitions used in the NIS2 Directive.

---

## Core System Definitions (1-4)

### (1) Network and Information System
Three components:
- **(a)** Electronic communications networks (per Directive 2018/1972)
- **(b)** Any device/group of devices that automatically process digital data via a programme
- **(c)** Digital data stored/processed/transmitted by the above

### (2) Security of Network and Information Systems
The ability to **resist events** that could compromise:
- **A**vailability
- **A**uthenticity
- **I**ntegrity
- **C**onfidentiality

Of data or services offered by those systems.

### (3) Cybersecurity
Defined by reference to Regulation (EU) 2019/881 (Cybersecurity Act).

### (4) National Cybersecurity Strategy
A coherent framework providing:
- Strategic objectives
- Priorities
- Governance mechanisms

---

## Incident Definitions (5-8)

### (5) Near Miss
An event that **could have** compromised availability, authenticity, integrity, or confidentiality but:
- Was successfully prevented, OR
- Did not materialise

### (6) Incident
An event that **actually compromises** availability, authenticity, integrity, or confidentiality of data or services.

### (7) Large-Scale Cybersecurity Incident
An incident that:
- Exceeds a single Member State's response capacity, OR
- Significantly impacts **2+ Member States**

### (8) Incident Handling
Actions and procedures for:
- **Prevent** - stop incidents before they occur
- **Detect** - identify when incidents happen
- **Analyse** - understand what happened
- **Contain** - limit the damage
- **Respond** - take action
- **Recover** - restore normal operations

---

## Risk & Threat Definitions (9-11)

### (9) Risk
**Formula**: Risk = Magnitude of loss × Likelihood of occurrence

### (10) Cyber Threat
Defined by Regulation (EU) 2019/881 (Cybersecurity Act).

### (11) Significant Cyber Threat
A cyber threat with potential to:
- Severely impact an entity's network/information systems
- Cause **considerable material or non-material damage** to users

---

## ICT Definitions (12-15)

### (12) ICT Product
Hardware/software products (see Reg. 2019/881)

### (13) ICT Service
Services delivered via ICT (see Reg. 2019/881)

### (14) ICT Process
Processes using ICT (see Reg. 2019/881)

### (15) Vulnerability
A **weakness, susceptibility, or flaw** in ICT products/services that can be exploited by a cyber threat.

---

## Standards Definitions (16-17)

### (16) Standard
Technical standard per EU standardization regulation (Reg. 1025/2012)

### (17) Technical Specification
Detailed technical requirements per EU standardization regulation

---

## Internet Infrastructure Definitions (18-22)

### (18) Internet Exchange Point (IXP)
A network facility that:
- Interconnects **2+ independent networks** (autonomous systems)
- Facilitates internet traffic exchange
- Only provides interconnection for autonomous systems
- Does NOT require traffic to pass through third parties
- Does NOT alter or interfere with traffic

### (19) Domain Name System (DNS)
A hierarchical distributed naming system that:
- Identifies internet services and resources
- Allows devices to use routing/connectivity services

### (20) DNS Service Provider
An entity providing:
- **(a)** Public recursive DNS resolution for end-users, OR
- **(b)** Authoritative DNS resolution for third parties

**Exception**: Root name servers are excluded.

### (21) Top-Level Domain (TLD) Name Registry
An entity that:
- Has been delegated a specific TLD (e.g., .com, .se, .eu)
- Administers the TLD including registration, technical operation, database maintenance

### (22) Entity Providing Domain Name Registration Services
Includes:
- **Registrars** (directly register domains)
- **Agents** acting on behalf of registrars
- Privacy/proxy registration providers
- Resellers

## Digital Service Definitions (23)

### (23) Digital Service
Defined by Directive (EU) 2015/1535 - Information Society services delivered electronically.

---

## Trust Services (24-27)

### (24) Trust Service
Electronic services for creating, verifying, validating electronic signatures, seals, timestamps, etc.

### (25) Trust Service Provider
Entity providing trust services

### (26) Qualified Trust Service
Trust service meeting eIDAS requirements for qualified status

### (27) Qualified Trust Service Provider
Provider meeting requirements for qualified status under eIDAS (Reg. 910/2014)

---

## Online Platform Definitions (28-29)

### (28) Online Marketplace
Platform allowing consumers to conclude contracts with traders online (per Directive 2005/29/EC)

### (29) Online Search Engine
Digital service allowing users to search websites based on queries (per Regulation 2019/1150)

---

## Cloud & Digital Infrastructure (30-33)

### (30) Cloud Computing Service
A digital service enabling:
- **On-demand administration**
- Broad remote access
- Scalable and elastic pool of **shareable computing resources**
- May be distributed across multiple locations

### (31) Data Centre Service
A service encompassing:
- Structures dedicated to centralised accommodation of IT/network equipment
- Data storage, processing, and transport services
- Power distribution facilities
- Environmental control infrastructure

### (32) Content Delivery Network (CDN)
A network of **geographically distributed servers** providing:
- High availability
- Accessibility
- Fast delivery of digital content/services

### (33) Social Networking Services Platform
A platform enabling users to:
- Connect, Share, Discover, Communicate
- Across multiple devices via chats, posts, videos, recommendations

---

## Entity & Administration Definitions (34-41)

### (34) Representative
A person/entity **established in the EU** designated to act on behalf of **non-EU entities** for NIS2 compliance purposes.

### (35) Public Administration Entity
An entity recognised by national law meeting ALL criteria:

| Criteria | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| **(a) Purpose** | Meeting general interest needs; NOT industrial/commercial |
| **(b) Legal status** | Has legal personality |
| **(c) Control** | Mainly state-financed OR state-supervised OR >50% board appointed by state |
| **(d) Powers** | Can make administrative/regulatory decisions affecting cross-border movement |

**Exclusions**: Judiciary, parliaments, central banks

### (36) Public Electronic Communications Network
Per Directive (EU) 2018/1972 (European Electronic Communications Code)

### (37) Electronic Communications Service
Per Directive (EU) 2018/1972

### (38) Entity
A natural or legal person that:
- Is created and recognised under national law
- Can exercise rights and be subject to obligations in its own name

### (39) Managed Service Provider (MSP)
An entity providing services for installation, management, operation, maintenance of ICT products, networks, infrastructure, applications, or information systems.

### (40) Managed Security Service Provider (MSSP)
An MSP specifically providing **cybersecurity risk management** services.

### (41) Research Organisation
Entity with primary goal of applied research or experimental development for commercial exploitation.
**Excludes**: Educational institutions.
