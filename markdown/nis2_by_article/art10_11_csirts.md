# NIS2 Directive - Articles 10 & 11: CSIRTs

## Article 10: CSIRT Establishment

### Article 10(1): Designation
- Each Member State must designate/establish **one or more CSIRTs**
- May be within a competent authority
- Must cover **all sectors in Annexes I and II**
- Responsible for incident handling

### Article 10(2-3): Resources & Infrastructure
- **Adequate resources** for Article 11(3) tasks
- Secure, resilient communication infrastructure
- Contribute to **secure information-sharing tools**

### Article 10(4-6): Cooperation
- Cooperate with sectoral/cross-sectoral communities
- Participate in **peer reviews** (Article 19)
- Ensure effective cooperation in **CSIRTs network**

### Article 10(7-8): International Cooperation
- May cooperate with **non-EU CSIRTs** using protocols like Traffic Light Protocol
- May provide cybersecurity assistance to third countries

### Article 10(9): Notification
Notify Commission of CSIRT identity without undue delay.

## Article 11: CSIRT Requirements & Tasks

### Article 11(1): Mandatory Requirements

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **(a) Communication** | High availability, no single points of failure, multiple contact methods |
| **(b) Physical security** | Premises and systems at secure sites |
| **(c) Request management** | Effective system for managing and routing requests |
| **(d) Operational security** | Confidentiality and trustworthiness |
| **(e) Staffing** | Adequate staffing for **24/7 availability**, trained staff |
| **(f) Business continuity** | Redundant systems, backup working space |

---

### Article 11(3): CSIRT Tasks

| Task | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **(a) Monitoring** | Monitor cyber threats, vulnerabilities, incidents at national level |
| **(b) Early Warning** | Provide warnings, alerts, announcements to entities (near real-time) |
| **(c) Incident Response** | Respond to incidents and assist entities |
| **(d) Forensics** | Collect/analyse forensic data, provide situational awareness |
| **(e) Vulnerability Scanning** | Proactive scanning upon request |
| **(f) Network Participation** | Participate in CSIRTs network, provide mutual assistance |
| **(g) Vulnerability Disclosure** | Act as coordinator (Article 12) |
| **(h) Information Sharing** | Contribute to secure information-sharing tools |

### Proactive Scanning Powers
CSIRTs **may** conduct **proactive non-intrusive scanning** of publicly accessible systems to detect vulnerabilities.
- Must NOT negatively impact entity services
- May use risk-based prioritization

### Article 11(4-5): Cooperation
- Establish cooperation with **private sector stakeholders**
- Promote common/standardized incident-handling practices
